Rapid Prototyping: 25 Portfolio Landing Pages

This project explores 25 different landing page designs for the same personal portfolio idea. The process moves from broad exploration to a refined final direction.

Convergence (v11 - v18)

Refinement (v19 - v25)

v19 v20 v21 v22 v23 v24 v25 ⭐

These versions refine spacing, typography, and structure to reach a final design.

Final Choice: v25

The final version represents a balance between clarity, structure, and visual identity. Earlier versions explored a wide range of directions, including minimal, brutalist, editorial, and playful styles. Through iteration, the design converged into a clean editorial layout that is both readable and expressive.
